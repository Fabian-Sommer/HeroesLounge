Heroes Cup by Emim

A Community tournament, presented by Emim Studio's with a total prizepool of 12.000 RUB.

General information:

The tournament is funded and ran by Emim studios. The tournament exists of two stages, and all EU based teams are free to sign up to participate.

Signups open on May 10th at midnight MSK (May 9th 23:00 CEST).

Signups close on May 23rd at 17:00 CEST / 18:00 MSK

Prize pool

The teams that reach the semi-finals have to play all matches to determine their prize pool.

Total prize pool: 12.000 RUB ~ $185

1st place: 6.000 RUB ~ $92
2nd place: 3.000 RUB ~ $46
3rd place: 2.000 RUB ~ $30
4th place: 1.000 RUB ~ $15

Live broadcasts will take place on the official youtube and twitch channel of Emim Studio.
