Heroes Lounge values the privacy of its players and visitors to its website. As such, we comply with the EU privacy law ‘GDPR’ (general data protection regulation).


In order to run the competition we use the following personal data of our registered users:

  • Blizzard BattleTag
  • Discord Tag
  • E-mail address

Players can request the deletion of this data if they want to leave the competition. Deletion of this data is not possible if a person wants to participate in the league.


We use third party software to communicate with our players. You can find their respective privacy policies here:

https://discordapp.com/privacy

https://mailchimp.com/legal/privacy/


About three times during an ongoing season we send a newsletter to users participating in the competition. Each newsletter has a button to unsubscribe from future newsletters. A similar button is also present on your own profile page.
