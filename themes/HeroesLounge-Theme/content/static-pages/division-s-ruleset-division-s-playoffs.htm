Heroes Lounge Division S Playoff format 


The Division S playoffs will take place on Saturday the 14th and Sunday the 15th of March for EU and Friday March 13th to Sunday March 15th in NA.

The top 6 teams from the league play will compete in the Division S playoffs. The playoffs will have a double elimination format, with Bo5 matches. 
For the finals, the team that comes from the winners bracket will start with First Pick and Map Pick in the Grand Final.
